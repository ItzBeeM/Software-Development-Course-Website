Lesson 4: Contact with Client

This lesson focuses on the importance of effective communication and collaboration with clients in software development projects. We will discuss strategies for gathering requirements, managing expectations, and maintaining a positive client-developer relationship throughout the project lifecycle. Talk about how clients sometimes don't know what they actually want.

You would think that understanding what the client wants is rather straiegthforward. In more traditional contex like for example carpentry, client gives the dimensions, the materials, the design and the carpenter just does what he is told. In software development it's not that simple, clients often don't know what they want, or even worse they change the requrments during the development process, and it's the developers job to understand what the client actually wants and to deliver a product that meets their needs. This can be a challenging task, as it requires good communication skills, empathy, and the ability to manage expectations.
