Lesson 1: Programming Lannguages

This lesson focuses on explaining differences between programming languages and their use cases. We will cover popular languages like Python, JavaScript, and Java, C, C++, etc., and discuss their strengths and weaknesses in various applications.

We typically classify languages into two groups: low level and high level. An example of a high level language is Python, it's often used for automation, machine learning, data analysis and webdev. These languages are pretty easy to understand, they have a high abstraction level they also have a lot of built in functions, but they are also typically slow in execution of code. On the other hand scripts written in low level languages are fast but also harder to write and less intuitive. You need to be careful with types of data and manual memory allocation. An example of such language would be C.
